Version 2.5
- Added Master Admin Panel to view all company jobs, manage users, and remotely wipe data.
- Introduced Job Importing: Admins can now clone jobs from other users to their own dashboard.
- Added Team Management: Create custom roles and assign team members directly to jobs or tasks.
- Added a global Dark Mode toggle in user settings.
- Added an Archiving system to safely hide completed jobs without permanently deleting them.
- Added Read-Only Share Links to safely send live job details to clients or sub-contractors.
